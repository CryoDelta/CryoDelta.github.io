People/ My Friends

I very much prefer my friends over other people, even a little over my family but I'd say it's a close fight.
I spend most of my time with friends, whether it's in real life or online, allowing them to know me better than anyone, making me feel safe with them.
I've never had trouble making friends so I would say I had good company at any time in my life.

Animals/ Cats

The only pet I ever had, or should I say my parents ever had, was a ginger cat with white stripes, kinda like a bootleg tiger.
His name was Saturn (like the planet that has the same color palette), and he was the cutest thing to ever live.
He would always come to us and ask for hugs, really, the perfect cat, how could i not love cats after that?

Food/ Spicy Food

People tend to think "Oh you like spicy food, are you a masochist or something?" but they will never understand that, with time, you get used to the level of spicyness.
Nowadays, most "spicy" food for the public is just good, tasty food for me.
I don't sweat eating that, I sweat eating indian or mexican food but, since I'm desensitized, I can just enjoy the taste of the food (spoiler, it's delicious).

Sports/ Badminton

I've tried a few sports...
First with football/soccer, I didn't like the fact that there was the possibility that one very good player could carry the whole team.
Then I got my hands on basketball, liked it but got bored of the size difference (I'm small): can't shoot, can't block, eh...
And, with badminton, I found the right balance of strategy, skill and strength that I need in sports.

Games/ Roguelites

As a child, I would play all types of games: adventure, RPGs, action...
But then, I got my hands on Dead Cells, and from there, I got addicted to the endless replayability and the constent rediscovery of roguelites.
DeadCells -> Moonlighter -> Hades, to name only the ones I played on Switch, and with Hades 2 coming out in 2023, it's not going to stop soon.
Oh, I play Riot's games too, for some reason.

Music/ OSTs

With all the cool games I play, it is obvious why my favorite genre of music are sountracks: most games have banger OSTs!
Like, I want to chill and travel? -> Moonlighter OST, want to get hyped? -> League of Legends music, want to push yourself? -> Hades score...
So flexible and if I want to get funny I can listen at random to my favorite OSTs and just have a soundtrack in my life, I love it.

People/ Idiots

I wouldn't call myself a genius, that would be a big lie, but I can't stand people so dumb and close minded that you can't have a logical debate with them.
Those people will bring up points from alternate dimensions, like, where the hell is that information coming from? Have you foreseen it in a dream?
Also the fact that "idiots" are more common than intelligent people, allowing them to win democratic elections influencing our lives, depresses me.

Animals/ Flying Bugs

For them, it's hate AND fear.
Casual crawling bugs are fine to me: I probably outrun them and can kill them easily if things go wrong.
For example, I really don't mind normal spiders but if even the smallest gnat comes in my face, I'll not stop until i know it's dead because flying bugs are frantic, noisy and, for the mosquito case, annoying.
The only exeptions are butterflies, with my favorite ones being moths.

Food/ Mushrooms

Mushrooms are devious little creatures, being up to mischief.
I associate them with parasotes and, just thinking of the zombifying mushrooms that feed on their dead host and force them to kill others only for the shroom to spread on, terrifies me and totally discards that evil thing from my diet.
Btw, the zombie mushrooms are real, with insects. Don't look it up, you'll get nighmares after.

Sports/ Running

I... don't understand people who run.
I mean, good for them, they stay fit, but really? Running after what? Nothing? Why? Do they enjoy feeling their body weakening and tiring for nothing?
Personally, I don't. I run after a ball, after the birdie or because I'm late but otherwise, no, I just walk to places, while listening to music, thinking of what I'll do next, chatting, etc...

Games/ Horror

As you may have seen from the mushroom fear or the bug fear, I'm very easily feared by something so obviously horror games are not made for me.
Horror, being physical or mental just doesn't appeal to me: I like being safe or at least aware of what I risk.
In horror media, the use of "jumpscares" is almost inevitable and with the more experienced horror consumer asking for more and more horrific experiences, i really don't have a choice but to avoid it.

Music/ Ultra Core genres
